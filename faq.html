FAQs

What is the purpose of this site?
This is my personal site where I can store my ramblings. It's inspired by the early ideals of the World Wide Web in which anyone could join the level playing field and have their voice heard.
What JavaScript framework did you use to build it?
Uhh... none! This is a static site, and I wanted to keep it small and snappy. It's just a small amount of HTML and CSS. I believe strongly in using the right tool for the job. Plus, I love how quickly HTML-only sites load. :)
