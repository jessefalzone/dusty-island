My Career, So Far

I've been a web developer for the past 10 years. My focus has been front-end development, but mostly recently I was Director of [Full-Stack] Application Development and then CTO.

Those were valuable roles and great learning experiences. I questiond a lot of it: How do I get people to enjoy standup meetings and update their tickets? What the heck are my DevOps engineers doing? How do I manage these people and these budgets? Do I even want to?

It took me a while to answer that last question but eventually it became a clear, "No". I was burned out. I realized I needed to go back to getting my hands dirty building cool things (both virtual and real) in a more creative space.
